Call for Expressions of Interest

The European Commission is launching a Call for Expressions of Interest to select 15 European cities with low to medium levels of digital maturity that will receive policy advice, coaching and support to speed up their digital transformation and industrial modernisation.

Participants will receive a set of hands-on and tailor-made advisory services including:

  • Advice and consultation from high-level experts with local and international experience in designing and implementing digital transformation strategies
  • Access to innovative training and collective learning at the Academy
  • Support in the development and implementation of a practical policy strategy
  • Help finding cooperation and funding opportunities
  • Access to European networks and platforms

The support and services offered will be delivered through a combination of local-level advisory services as well as through the organisation of collective Academy seminars.

Target Audience

The call for Expression of Interest is addressed to local public organisations from European cities willing to become model demonstrators of digital transformation and industrial modernisation. This refers to local authorities or their representative public agency.

Objectives

The four key objectives of the initiative are:

To foster local innovation ecosystems through the provision of high-level professional expertise and advisory services for model regions and cities of digital transformation and industrial modernisation
To reinforce the pan-European networking of the model regions and cities with the 13 case studies of the Blueprint and other regions and cities, to promote effective coordination and potential cross-border investments
To facilitate stakeholder collaboration and stimulate investments, by exploring and developing potential cross-regional partnerships, designing common projects and attracting private investments for digital transformation of industrial value chains
To identify the main obstacles, success factors and challenges for the creation of innovation ecosystems, with a view to continuously upgrading the Blueprint for regions and cities of digital transformation and industrial modernisation
